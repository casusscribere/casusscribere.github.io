### Conference Panel — Florida Historical Society 2025 Annual Meeting & Symposium, Orlando, FL

*With Pamela Schwartz and Connie Lester.*

Panelists discuss two exhibits, one digital one physical, which tell the story of the Ocoee massacre; the discussion centers on issues of accessibility, cultural sensitivity, and archival challenges related to source documentation.
