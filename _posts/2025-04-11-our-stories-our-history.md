### Public-Facing Scholarship — April 11, 2025

*With Connie Lester and Geoffrey Cravero.*

A presentation for the University of Central Florida's Celebrates the Arts annual public-facing presentation series. The panelists discuss the design of digital data visualizations specifically addressing the history of black communities in central Florida.
